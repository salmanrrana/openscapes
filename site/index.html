A private score for this browser

OpenScapes listens sideways.

Load time, screen shape, pointer drift, and optional room light become a slow ambient field. The browser becomes the instrument.

The composition reads no-permission hints: timing, connection quality when exposed, device shape, clock drift, performance jitter, and touch or mouse motion.

It does not inspect requests, Wi-Fi traffic, history, files, or private data. Camera light joins only when you start the Light instrument.

Click or touch anywhere after entry. Every pixel is a seed: x changes pan and pitch, y changes brightness and decay.
